For Schools & Institutions

Preparing the Next Generation for an AI Shaped World

AI tools are already part of how students learn, research, and produce work often without structured guidance on how to use them ethically or effectively.

Our AI Ethics Awareness Workshops equip students with foundational knowledge and practical awareness to engage with AI responsibly in the classroom and beyond.

  • Introduction to AI what it is, how it works, and where it appears in everyday life
  • Responsible use of AI tools in education and academic integrity
  • Understanding AI generated content and evaluating outputs critically
  • Risks of AI misinformation, bias, privacy, and ethical boundaries
  • Ethical digital behaviour habits for responsible online participation
