Policy

How Linnexus Digital Solutions collects, uses, and protects your data.

1. Introduction

Linnexus Digital Solutions Nig Ltd ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your personal information when you visit our website or use our AI consulting and education services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you explicitly provide to us (e.g., name, email, company name) when you fill out our contact or training registration forms. We may also passively collect basic analytics data (like IP address, browser type) to improve our website experience.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your information exclusively to provide our services, respond to your inquiries, administer workshops and certifications, and occasionally send relevant updates. We never sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Data Security

We implement strict technical and organizational measures to protect your data. Since we emphasize responsible AI governance, adhering to high standards of data security is at the core of our operations.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy or your data, please email us at info@linnexus.com.
